Rescue Guide

  • 1

    Assess the Situation

    Check if the animal is truly in distress. Some animals may not need intervention.

  • 2

    Ensure Your Safety

    Approach carefully. Injured animals may bite or scratch out of fear.

  • 3

    Contain the Animal

    Use a blanket or carrier to safely transport the animal if possible.

  • 4

    Contact Professionals

    Reach out to local animal control, wildlife rehabilitators, or vets.
