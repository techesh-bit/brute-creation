Basic Animal Care

  • Proper Nutrition

    Learn about species-specific dietary needs and provide fresh food and water daily.

  • Veterinary Care

    Regular check-ups and vaccinations are essential for animal health.

  • Safe Environment

    Create a comfortable, hazard-free living space for your pet.

  • Exercise & Stimulation

    Provide physical activity and mental enrichment daily.

Rescue Guidance

  • Approaching Animals

    Move slowly and speak softly to avoid frightening injured animals.

  • Emergency Kit

    Keep a rescue kit with blanket, carrier, gloves, and first aid supplies.

  • Local Resources

    Know your local wildlife rehabilitators and animal shelters.

  • Safety First

    Never endanger yourself - call professionals for dangerous situations.

How You Can Help

  • Volunteer

    Offer your time at local shelters or rescue organizations.

  • Foster

    Provide temporary homes for animals in transition.

  • Educate

    Share knowledge about responsible pet ownership and wildlife protection.

  • Advocate

    Support animal welfare legislation and report cruelty.
